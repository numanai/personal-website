Research Summary

I mainly do research in the field of applied machine learning and deep learning. I am currently involved in modeling booking and ancillary revenues for Etihad Airways. Previously, I worked on solving some of the nonlinear problems in the field of infra-red thermography using machine learning. I developed a platform for detecting defects in composite structures from thermograms and estimating their depths using computer vision models. I also worked on the design and characterization of a MEMS device called pMUT. This device is a tiny ultrasonic transducer that has applications in the medical field mainly, but not limited to it. The ideas behind unsupervised learning and reinforcement learning fascinates me as they resemble the autonomy observed in human-beings. Thus, I am planning to dig more into these topics in the future.
