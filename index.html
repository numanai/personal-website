About Me

I am a Senior Data Analyst at the Enterprise Analytics Department in Etihad Airways, Abu Dhabi, UAE. I automate processes, forecast bookings and visualize data. I received my fully-funded MSc. degree from Khalifa University/Masdar Institute (UAE) in Microsystems Engineering and my BSc. degree from FAST-NUCES in Electrical Engineering (Pakistan) in 2016 and 2013 respectively. For my MSc. thesis, I worked with Dr. Jaime Viegas on characterizing pMUT devices, those are tiny MEMS devices that are used in your iPhone fingerprint identification. I also worked as a Research Engineer with Dr. Mohamad Omar on using machine learning models to locate defects in composite structures and estimate their depth. In my free time, I like to read history and science books and to play cricket. I drink one cup of coffee a day, before 12 P.M not to disturb my circadian cycle.
